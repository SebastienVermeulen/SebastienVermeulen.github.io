Madmax styled bot

Robot retopologized, weighted and textured in substance painter and 3DSMax

A robot in the style of the late 70's / early 80's MadMax movies.
The colour scheme comes from the Ford XB Falcon - 'Persuit Interceptor'.

I liked the colourfullness, contrasted with the dusty and neglected state of the machine.

The creation

A bot with bad low-poly topology had to be optimised so that it would be more usable in games as well as easier to texture. After that all the bones had to be weighted again so that it could be posed properly. This all took place in 3DSMax.

Afterwards in Substance Painter a texture was painted on top of it. Together with a normal map and roughness map. Lastly an AO was generated for it.

When this was all done the Unreal Engine 4 was used to produce some beauty shots of the mech, with the final result visible below.
